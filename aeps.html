Digital Seva Kendra AePS Service

AEPS is a payment system in India that allows bank customers to access their Aadhaar-linked bank accounts and perform basic banking transactions such as cash withdrawals, balance inquiries, and fund transfers using their Aadhaar number and biometric authentication. AEPS services are typically offered by banks and financial institutions in India.

The National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) launched the Aadhaar Enabled Payment System (AePS) to promote cashless transactions. For biometric verification, the AePS service utilizes a fingerprint or iris scan, guaranteeing excellent security for each transaction.
